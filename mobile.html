Who are we?

We, Fred and Evan, are two sophomores at Portsmouth High School, RI, who sell raw New England honey in our off-time. We are committed
to fighting the extinction of the honey bee and to bringing a quality product to the people of Rhode Island; we run this business using our own
funds and donate ten percent of all profits to help save the bees!


Why buy our honey?

When you buy Fred and Evan's honey, you can feel great knowing that the only two places your money is going is directly into the growth of a
small local business, and straight to the Save The Bees fund. Not to mention, it tastes great! When you buy our honey, you're not getting any old
run-of-the-mill supermarket honey, you're getting locally hand-packaged, 100% raw, delicious, New England honey. There are dozens of reasons
to try Fred and Evan's, so pick up a jar today!
